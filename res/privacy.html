隐私承诺

Privacy Commitment

心迹（ThoughtEcho）是一款本地优先的笔记应用。我们始终坚持最小化数据收集原则，绝大部分功能均在用户本地设备上完成。我们不收集、不存储、不向我们的服务器传输您的任何笔记内容或个人识别信息。

ThoughtEcho is a local-first note-taking application. We strictly adhere to the principle of data minimization. Most features are executed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your note content or personally identifiable information to our servers.

数据存储与处理

Data Storage and Processing

1. 本地存储

1. Local Storage

  • 笔记与媒体：存储于设备本地 SQLite 数据库中。
  • Notes & Media: Stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • 配置信息：通过 MMKV 或 SharedPreferences 存储于本地。
  • Configurations: Stored locally via MMKV or SharedPreferences.
  • 加密存储：AI 服务的 API 密钥通过系统级加密方案（flutter_secure_storage）保护。
  • Encrypted Storage: API keys for AI services are protected using system-level encryption (flutter_secure_storage).

2. 不收集的信息

2. Information We Do Not Collect

我们明确声明不收集以下信息：

We explicitly declare that we do not collect:

  • 用户个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、电话等）。
  • Personally Identifiable Information (name, email, phone, etc.).
  • 应用遥测、分析或使用行为追踪数据。
  • App telemetry, analytics, or usage tracking data.
  • 设备标识符（IMEI、MAC 地址等）。
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.).

第三方服务与 API

Third-Party Services and APIs

为了提供特定功能，应用可能会调用以下第三方服务，这可能涉及数据传输：

To provide specific features, the app may call the following third-party services, which involves data transfer:

1. AI 服务（OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek 等）

1. AI Services (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, etc.)

  • 触发机制：仅在用户主动发起 AI 请求时触发。
  • Trigger: Only activated when the user explicitly initiates an AI request.
  • 数据传输：相关的笔记上下文会通过加密连接发送至您配置的服务商。
  • Data Transfer: Relevant note context is sent via an encrypted connection to your configured provider.
  • 隐私约束：此类数据处理受各服务商自身隐私政策约束。
  • Privacy Terms: Such data processing is subject to the respective provider's privacy policy.

2. 地理位置与天气服务

2. Location and Weather Services

  • Open-Meteo：用于获取当前天气信息，涉及发送地理坐标。
  • Open-Meteo: Used to fetch weather info, involves sending geographical coordinates.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim：用于将坐标转换为地址描述或搜索城市。
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: Used for reverse geocoding or city searching.

3. 内容同步 (LocalSend 协议)

3. Content Sync (LocalSend Protocol)

  • 应用支持通过局域网在设备间同步数据。该过程为点对点传输，不经过任何云端服务器。
  • The app supports data sync between devices via local network. This is a peer-to-peer transfer and does not pass through any cloud servers.

您的控制权

Your Control

  • 权限管理：您可以随时在系统设置中撤回位置、相机等权限授权。
  • Permissions: You can revoke permissions (location, camera, etc.) at any time in system settings.
  • 数据导出与删除：您可以随时导出本地备份，或通过清除应用数据来永久删除所有内容。
  • Data Management: You can export local backups or permanently delete all content by clearing app data.

儿童隐私

Children's Privacy

本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，我们不会主动收集此类人群的任何信息。

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
